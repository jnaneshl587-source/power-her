Women Safety Tips

Travel Safety

Always share your live location with trusted contacts when traveling alone. Avoid isolated routes, especially after dark.

Digital Safety

Keep your device charged and carry a power bank. Use discreet safety apps that can be activated quickly in emergencies.

Public Transport

Note down vehicle numbers before boarding. Sit near the driver in taxis or use women-only compartments when available.

Self-Defense

Learn basic self-defense techniques. Carry legal self-defense tools like pepper spray where permitted.

Social Awareness

Trust your instincts. If a situation feels unsafe, remove yourself immediately. Be aware of your surroundings.

Emergency Preparedness

Save emergency contacts on speed dial. Know the emergency numbers for your location and have a safety plan.

Safety Training

Access free online self-defense tutorials and safety awareness courses designed specifically for women.

What to Do During Emergency

Stay Calm

Take a deep breath and try to remain as calm as possible. Panic can cloud your judgment.

Press SOS

Immediately press the SOS button on the app to alert your emergency contacts and authorities.

Find Safe Location

Move to a well-lit, public area if possible. Avoid isolated spots.

Call Emergency

Dial 112 or your local emergency number if you're able to make a call.

Contact Us

Get in touch with us for support, feedback, or emergency assistance

About Nirbhaya App

Nirbhaya is a comprehensive women safety application designed to provide security and peace of mind. Our mission is to create a safer environment for women through technology.

The app features real-time location tracking, emergency contact alerts, safety information, and an instant SOS button that notifies your trusted contacts and nearby authorities in case of emergency.

With Nirbhaya, you're never alone. Our advanced safety features work together to ensure help is always within reach when you need it most.
