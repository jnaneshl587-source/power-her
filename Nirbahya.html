Women Safety Guide

Comprehensive safety information and preventive measures to ensure your security in various situations.

Travel Safety

  • Always share your live location with trusted contacts when traveling alone.
  • Plan your route in advance and stick to well-lit, populated areas.
  • Avoid isolated routes, especially after dark.
  • Keep your phone charged and carry a power bank at all times.
  • Note down vehicle numbers before boarding taxis or rideshares.
  • Sit near the driver in taxis or use women-only compartments when available.

Home Safety

  • Ensure all doors and windows are securely locked, especially at night.
  • Install safety devices like door chain locks and peepholes.
  • Keep emergency numbers saved on speed dial.
  • Don't open the door to strangers without proper identification.
  • Have a safety plan in case of emergency situations.
  • Keep a self-defense tool like pepper spray in accessible locations.

Personal Safety

  • Learn basic self-defense techniques for emergency situations.
  • Trust your instincts - if a situation feels unsafe, remove yourself immediately.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid distractions like phones while walking.
  • Carry legal self-defense tools where permitted.
  • Keep emergency contacts on speed dial and memorize important numbers.
  • Use discreet safety apps that can be activated quickly in emergencies.

Emergency Preparedness

  • Save emergency contacts on speed dial and memorize important numbers.
  • Know the emergency numbers for your specific location.
  • Have a safety plan and share it with trusted family or friends.
  • Keep important documents and emergency cash in a safe, accessible place.
  • Practice emergency scenarios so you can react quickly if needed.
  • Keep emergency medical information readily available.

Digital Safety

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication where available.
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information online.
  • Regularly update your privacy settings on social media.
  • Install and maintain security software on your devices.
  • Be aware of location sharing settings in apps and social media.
